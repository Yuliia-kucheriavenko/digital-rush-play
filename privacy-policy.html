Privacy Policy

Welcome to Digital Rush Play! We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our store.

1. Information We Collect

We collect data to improve your experience and fulfill your orders:

  • Personal details (name, email, address)
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party providers)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Technical data (IP address, device type, browser info)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information for purposes such as:

  • Processing orders and delivering game keys
  • Sending order confirmations and updates
  • Personalizing offers and content
  • Improving site functionality and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for payments, analytics, and email delivery. These providers only receive the information needed to perform their functions.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences, provide targeted content, and analyze traffic.

5. Your Privacy Choices

You have control over your personal data:

  • Access or update your personal information at any time
  • Opt-out of promotional emails
  • Adjust cookie preferences in your browser settings

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Users

Digital Rush Play is based in Australia, but we welcome gamers worldwide. By using our site, you agree to the transfer and processing of your data in accordance with this policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a revised date at the top of the page.
