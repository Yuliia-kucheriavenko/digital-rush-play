Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Digital Rush Play uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit or use our website. By continuing to browse, you agree to our use of cookies as described below.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, enhance functionality, and improve your user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., cart, login).
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the site (e.g., analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences (e.g., language or location).
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to show relevant ads and track the effectiveness of campaigns.

3. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure secure and smooth browsing
  • Personalize your shopping experience
  • Analyze traffic and improve our services
  • Deliver relevant offers and content

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by trusted third parties like Google Analytics or Meta Pixel. These tools help us measure performance and improve your overall experience.

5. Cookie Duration

Cookies may be session-based (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent (stored for a longer period). Duration depends on the cookie type and purpose.

6. Your Cookie Choices

You can choose to accept or reject cookies. Most browsers allow you to manage your cookie settings or block cookies entirely via the settings menu.

7. How to Control Cookies

You may:

  • Use browser tools to delete or block cookies
  • Opt-out of third-party analytics tools (e.g., Google opt-out plugin)
  • Disable tracking cookies in your privacy settings

8. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Blocking cookies may limit some features on our website, including shopping cart, login functionality, and personalization tools.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
